Help

Back

To ride the peter pan you need to buy a token. To use the token you need to activate it. Once activated, your token is only valid for 30 min, and only for single-ride use.

To set a notification, tap the time of the bus you are planning on taking. Confirm this is the time you would like to set a notification for.

Collect rewards as you buy tokens on myPeter. 4 tokens until your next free ride!
